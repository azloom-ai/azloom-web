Our Story

Where Nature Meets
Pure Comfort

Villas Macondo is a boutique hotel built around a lush pool oasis, just 200 meters from world-famous Tamarindo beach — where Costa Rica's best surf meets breathtaking Pacific sunsets.

Wake up to the singing of tropical birds and howler monkeys echoing through the trees. Here, every morning reminds you that you are exactly where you should be.

Whether you're here to surf, explore, or simply breathe, Villas Macondo is your base camp for the Pura Vida life.
